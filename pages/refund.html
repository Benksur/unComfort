REFUND POLICY

Effective Date: 21.05.2025


At uncomfort., we value your satisfaction and want to ensure you have a positive experience. Our Refund Policy outlines our commitment to providing a hassle-free return process for our customers.


1. Return Policy


Customers may return items for a refund within 14 days of receipt, provided the items are in their original condition and packaging.

Items must be returned in their original packaging and with all original tags and labels intact.

Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days of receiving the returned item.


2. Return Process


Initiate a return through our website or email support@uncomfort.co.

Include your order number and reason for return in the return request.

We will provide you with a return label and instructions for returning the item.


3. Return Conditions


Items must be returned in their original condition and packaging.

Items must be returned within 14 days of receipt.

Items must be returned with all original tags and labels intact.


4. Return Processing


Once we receive your returned item, we will process your refund within 7-10 business days.

Refunds will be processed in the same payment method as your original purchase.


5. Non-Refundable Items


Final sale items are non-refundable.

Customized or personalized items are non-refundable.


6. Contact Us


Questions? Reach out to support@uncomfort.co or use our contact form.
