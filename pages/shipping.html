SHIPPING POLICY

Effective Date: 21.05.2025


At uncomfort., we value your satisfaction and want to ensure you have a positive experience. Our Shipping Policy outlines our commitment to providing fast and reliable delivery for our customers.


1. Shipping Options


Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days.

Express Shipping: 1-2 business days.

During high-volume periods, there may be slight delays.


2. Shipping Costs


Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight and size of the item.

Shipping costs are non-refundable.


3. Customs and Duties


Customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient.

Customs clearance may take up to 14 days.


4. Lost or Damaged Packages


If you receive a lost or damaged package, please contact us immediately at support@uncomfort.co.

We will work with you to resolve the issue and provide a replacement or refund.


5. Contact Us


Questions? Reach out to support@uncomfort.co or use our contact form.
